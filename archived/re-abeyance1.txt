ABEYANCE1 functions less as a musical composition than as a physiological protocol, designed to induce Hypofrontality—the temporary suspension of the brain’s executive functions—through the systematic exhaustion of metabolic resources. Eschewing traditional form, the work follows an Inverse Sawtooth Gradient: high-entropy "Rev" phases progressively contract while restorative "Drop" phases expand. This structure leverages the "Dysrhythmic Exhaustion" hypothesis, driving the brain to its metabolic limit to entice a homeostatic downshift into trance. The sonic architecture rests on two opposing forces: the electronic "Stressor," which induces Gamma saturation, and the acoustic "Safety Signal," where the ensemble’s pure sonorities provide refuge from the calculated dissonance.

The protocol unfolds in four distinct neurological stages. The ‘initialization’ phase explore the limits of Working Memory as defined by Miller’s Law. By deploying six high-velocity vocal trajectories alongside stochastic visual glitches, the piece encourages the brain to track variables beyond its processing capacity. This triggers a Buffer Overflow, engaging the Orienting Reflex without resolution and rapidly burning through the listener's Executive Attention. Next, the ‘Binding Fracture’ trips the Gamma-Epsilon Switch via Photic Driving and Binaural Roughness. By forcing the cortex into Gamma oscillation through 40Hz pitch shifts, the mechanism fractures the neural binding responsible for unified perception; the subsequent drop precipitates a plummet to slow-wave stabilization, mimicking the onset of deep sleep.

As the piece deepens, The ‘Semantic Crash’ targets Wernicke’s Exhaustion through extreme Information Entropy. By flashing text at 15Hz while the ensemble transmits high-density phonemes, the protocol overwhelms the brain's language processing centers. Unable to decode the stream, Wernicke’s Area shuts down to prevent metabolic burnout, inducing a state of absolute "Mental Silence." Finally, the ‘Fractionation’ phase attempts to secure the trance. “Conditioned” to as a biological necessity, the audience may experience a release of endogenous opioids, behavior consistent with Safety Signal Theory. As the final "Rev" generates vestibular dissonance through a Z-Axis Spiral, the subsequent ‘Program upload’ may speak more to one’s survival mechanism, no longer perceived merely as music.
